Drone project > Drone project > Operational Analysis > Operational Capabilities > Operative mission support The client requires coordinated support for field missions with real‑time guidance.

The client needs the system to support field operations by coordinating resource allocation, tactical planning, and real‑time communication between command centers and field units. For example, during an active interdiction mission, the system must synthesize data from surveillance, detection, and intelligence sources to deliver continuous updates and actionable guidance to operatives, enabling dynamic adjustments and ensuring mission objectives are achieved efficiently.
