Drone project > Drone project > Operational Analysis > Operational Capabilities > Active search The client requires proactive search operations in intelligence‑flagged regions.

The client needs to actively search within intelligence‑flagged areas to uncover illicit activities that might evade continuous monitoring. For example, if intelligence suggests that a new smuggling route is emerging off the coast of Galicia, the system must be capable of initiating targeted search operations—such as focused radar sweeps or UAV-assisted inspections—to locate hidden vessels. This proactive approach ensures comprehensive coverage and enhances the overall detection of covert or low‑visibility targets.
