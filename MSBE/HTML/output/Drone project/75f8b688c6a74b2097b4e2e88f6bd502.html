Identification and tracking support

OperationalCapability

Drone project > Drone project > Operational Analysis > Operational Capabilities > Identification and tracking support The client requires processing and integration of external identification and tracking requests.

The client needs the system to efficiently process and integrate external requests for vessel identification and tracking, such as those from intelligence platforms like SIVE. This entails validating, prioritizing, and incorporating these requests into the operational workflow. For instance, when a request is received to identify a vessel in a suspected smuggling area, the system must quickly gather and process the relevant detection data to provide a timely, coordinated response.
