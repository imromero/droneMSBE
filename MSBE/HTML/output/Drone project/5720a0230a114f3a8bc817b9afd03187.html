Preserve the integrity of the evidence.

OperationalCapability

Drone project > Drone project > Operational Analysis > Operational Capabilities > Preserve the integrity of the evidence. The client requires that all evidence remains secure, unaltered, and accessible.

The client needs the system to preserve the integrity of all collected evidence by ensuring it remains in its original, unaltered state. This involves implementing robust security measures, including encrypted storage, strict access controls, and chain‑of‑custody procedures. For example, each piece of evidence must be digitally timestamped and stored in a tamper‑proof repository, guaranteeing that it remains authentic and admissible for forensic investigations and legal actions.
