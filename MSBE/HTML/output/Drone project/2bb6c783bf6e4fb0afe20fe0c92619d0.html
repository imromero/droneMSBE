Collect evidences

OperationalCapability

Drone project > Drone project > Operational Analysis > Operational Capabilities > Collect evidences The client requires systematic collection of all relevant operational evidence.

The client needs the system to systematically capture and archive all operational evidence during surveillance activities. This includes high‑resolution images, video recordings, radar outputs, and sensor readings. For example, during an interdiction operation, every critical detail—from the initial detection of a vessel to its subsequent tracking—should be automatically recorded and stored in a centralized database, providing a comprehensive record for forensic analysis and legal proceedings.
