Vessel identification

OperationalCapability

Drone project > Drone project > Operational Analysis > Operational Capabilities > Vessel identification The client requires automated classification and registration of vessels.

The client needs to identify vessels automatically by classifying them based on key attributes such as size, shape, color, and registration data. For example, if a vessel’s characteristics match those of a vessel previously linked to smuggling, the system should promptly flag it for further investigation. This requirement enables rapid differentiation between benign vessels and potential threats, enhancing decision‑making accuracy and reducing reliance on manual identification methods.
